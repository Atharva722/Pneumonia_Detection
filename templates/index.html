Pneumonia is a serious respiratory infection that affects the lungs' air sacs (alveoli). When infected, these air sacs can fill with fluid or pus, causing symptoms such as:

  • Difficulty breathing
  • Chest pain when breathing or coughing
  • Cough with phlegm production
  • Fever and chills

Early detection is crucial for effective treatment. Our deep learning model analyzes chest X-ray images to help identify potential cases of pneumonia with high accuracy.
